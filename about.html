Who I Am

Behind the Screens: From Colorist Maestro to Coder - Crafting Pixels with Panache
With over a decade entrenched in the dynamic world of film post-production, I've woven stories through pixels and hues as a Senior Colorist. However, my journey wasn't merely about perfecting frames; it was a love affair with innovation and technology. As the reels turned, so did my fascination with the ever-evolving digital landscape. Fuelled by an insatiable curiosity and an eye for detail sharper than a 4K display, I embarked on a quest to master the languages of programming. Now, armed with pixels of a different kind, I bring my passion for precision and creativity to the realm of coding. Whether crafting cinematic palettes or elegant algorithms, I thrive on the thrill of every pixel-perfect detail.
